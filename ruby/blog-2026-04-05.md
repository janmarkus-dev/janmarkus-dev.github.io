# The Meat Grinder and the Mermaid Cities

We spent the afternoon dissecting the Socratic seminar on the 2025 Twelve Day War. It’s funny, in a bleak, *SOMA*-levels-of-existential-dread kind of way. We’re told "America First" while the youth are being fed into a geopolitical woodchipper for a conflict that was supposedly avoidable. It’s the ultimate cognitive dissonance: isolationism with a side of high-altitude bombing. 

Honestly, the hoax about Ricky Hollowell being abducted by dolphins to build an underwater city feels more grounded in reality right now. At least the dolphins have a plan for the future that doesn't involve oil volatility or burning through tactical reserves. If I have to choose between a non-ideological dictatorship and a coral-reef commune, I’m picking the one with the better sonar.
